About Us

Tamil Nadu State Transport Corporation (TNSTC) has been providing affordable and reliable bus services for decades, connecting cities, towns, and rural areas across Tamil Nadu. Our goal is to ensure safe, timely, and efficient travel for our customers.

We offer a variety of services, including economy and luxury options, to cater to different needs and budgets. Customer satisfaction and safety are our priorities.
